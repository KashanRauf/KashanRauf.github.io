General Information
Raha International School (RIS) is a private school near Raha Gardens in Abu Dhabi, UAE. RIS is the first school in Abu Dhabi authorized to teach the International Baccalaureate school system (EY, PYP, MYP, and DP systems), the school also holds an Outstanding rating given by ADEK. RIS was initially opened by Taaleem in 2006. RIS has announced the opening of a new campus in 2020 in Khalifa City next to the Khalifa City A school and Central Mall, this new campus will be leaning further towards sports than arts like the current campus.
Currently the school teaches over 2000 students from 80 different nationalities, with a university acceptance rate of 93% all over the world in many countries including Canada, Australia, Germany, United Kingdom, Japan, the United States, and more.
