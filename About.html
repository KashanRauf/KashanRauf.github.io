Early Days
RIS was initially opened in 2006 by Taaleem and authorized by ADEK. As of February 2009, they were given authority by the IB to teach the EY, PYP, MYP, and DP programs, since then the school has strived to make their vision a reality by providing excellent education and help every individual student succeed, and raising a community following the IB learner profile. In the past 13 years the school has grown in popularity and quality exponentially, as such it has earned many awards such as the "Outstanding" award by ADEK who do inspections yearly and rate schools as such.
Future
The school is currently in development of a new campus in Khalifa City, this second campus is expected to open in September 2020 to cater to the overflowing demand for spaces in the school by adding space for 3000 students from Kindergarten to grade 12, the campus is located near to the Khalifa City A School and Central Mall and has been designed by award-winning architects from Singapore, Architects CPG. While the current campus has a focus on the arts, this new campus is built and designed for sporting excellence.
