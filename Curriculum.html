About the IB
The IB (International Baccalaureate) is an international education system founded in 1968 offering 4 programs, EYP (early years program), PYP (pre-years program), MYP (middle years program), DP (diploma program). The IB is an often difficult and rigorous program, especially the diploma program, but is recognized by universities and colleges worldwide for how well it prepares students for their college years. As of May 2016 the IB is taught in 4460 schools and 151 territories worldwide.
